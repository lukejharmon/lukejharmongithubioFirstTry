GEIGER

I am the main author and maintainer of GEIGER, an R package for the comparative analysis of evolutionary radiations. The most current version of the software can be downloaded from CRAN or installed from within R. The NESCent phyloinformatics group has produced a HelpWiki that gives basic and advanced instructions for using R in comparative biology.
