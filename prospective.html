Information for prospective students

Undergraduates at the University of Idaho

I'm always looking for students to work independently on small projects in the lab. I'm especially interested in students with good math or computer backgrounds. I'm also supportive of local field projects involving herps, but you might have to come up with an idea for a project yourself! Contact me for more information.

Graduate Students

I will usually consider qualified PhD students to join the Harmon lab. Email or call me (208-885-0346) if you're interested. Our application deadline is Dec. 15. I am especially interested in students with experience in computer programming and statistics.

Postdoctoral Researchers

I don't currently have funding for new postdocs but would consider someone who has an idea to apply for funding.
