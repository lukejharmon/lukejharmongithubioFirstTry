Graduate students

Travis Hagey started his PhD in Fall 2007. His project involves the ecology, evolution, and functional morphology of geckos.
Denim Jochimsen started her PhD in Summer 2008. She is carrying out a project on Polynesian herps.
Simone DesRoches started her PhD in Fall 2008. Her project focuses on the evolutionary ecology of lizards in White Sands, New Mexico. Simone is co-advised by Erica Rosenblum.
Matt Pennell joined the lab in Fall 2010. He is interested in statistical comparative methods and macroevolution.
Kayla Hardwick started her PhD in Fall 2009. Her project is about behavior, selection, and speciation in White Sands lizards. Kayla is co-advised by Erica Rosenblum.
Daniel Caetano started his PhD in Fall 2012. He is working on comparative methods for studying trait evolution.
