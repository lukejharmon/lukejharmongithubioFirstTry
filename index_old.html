Welcome to the Harmon Lab

Our research investigates ecological and evolutionary aspects of adaptive radiations. We are interested in the causes and effects of both speciation and trait change, and how species interactions shape macroevolution. Most projects in the lab involve lizards, but Harmon lab research spans a wide range of taxa and time scales, from diversification in E. coli biofilms to macroevolutionary dynamics across the tree of life.
